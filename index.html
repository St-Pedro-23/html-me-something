    • Celia

    Celia is a human worshier of Pharasma, the goddess responsible for ushering the souls of the dead to their destinations. Celia considers putting the undead to rest a most holy duty. When not using her divine powers to heal injured party memembers, her weapon of choice is her trusty mace.

    Dez

    Dez is a Half-Orc battle wizard. Though she focuses her spells on fire, she has a wide breath of knowledge including; engineering, navigation, and even ancient languages. Not having grown up in either Orc or Half-Orc society, Dez finds a deep connection with those cultures and strives to learn as much she can about them.

    Dhash

    Divo Dhash (aka The Water Bandit, aka The Purple Perp, aka The Salty Skipper, aka The Dandy Lion) is the renound Undine musician and captain of the Defiant Verse. As a Watersinger his songs have been know to sway both his audience and the waters around him. Though he is rarely seen without his trusty cutlass (and several hand-crossbows), Divo Dhash maintains that, "Music is my weapon baby."

    Id'ma

    The most recent additoin to the party, Id'ma (aka Ravensong), is a Half-Orc warrioir from a small river side village. Id'ma grew up in a Half-Orc tribe where she was constantly reminded of her destiny as the mythic Ravensong, a warrior without peer who will ensure the survival of generations. She struggles to live up to this prophecy.

    Rajit

    Renound ratfolk trader Rajit, has been working with the party since the event at the docks. He helps to keep the party's buisness and trading efforts legitimate (to the best of his abilities). He owns a charming shop in Dockway where he conducts much of his business. In battle Rajit prefers to use his size to his advantage and tends to use his favorite dagger to pierce enemy weak spots.

    Magnimar

    Magnimar, (aka the City of Monuments, aka the City of Secrets), is one of Korvosa's largest cities and has a rich and complex history. Ever since the incident at the docks, Magnimar, particularly the area known as Dockway, serves as the party's defacto hometown/homeport.

    Moblin's

    Even before the incident at the docks, Moblin's served as the Dastardly Dockers's favorite spot in town. Most of the party has (semi)permamnt rooms there, they eat most of thier food there, find most of their jobs there, and Divo Dhash has regular shows there. Moblin's serves as a kind of defacto homebase for the party.

    The Defiant Verse

    The ship now known as the Defiant Verse has had many names in the past. It was last known as the Archnae, and was aquired by the current owners from a horrific captain known as the Wight Spider. Since then the Defiant Verse has gone on various adventures and has begun to gain a reputation as a fast and nimble ship that should not be triffled with.
